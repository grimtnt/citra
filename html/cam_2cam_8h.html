struct Service::CAM::Resolution

Resolution parameters for the camera.

The native resolution of 3DS camera is 640 * 480. The captured image will be cropped in the region [crop_x0, crop_x1] * [crop_y0, crop_y1], and then scaled to size width * height as the output image. Note that all cropping coordinates are inclusive.
