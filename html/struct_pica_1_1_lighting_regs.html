◆ LightingConfig

enum Pica::LightingRegs::LightingConfig : u32
strong

Pica fragment lighting supports using different LUTs for each lighting component: Reflectance R, G, and B channels, distribution function for specular components 0 and 1, fresnel factor, and spotlight attenuation.

Furthermore, which LUTs are used for each channel (or whether a channel is enabled at all) is specified by various pre-defined lighting configurations. With configurations that require more LUTs, more cycles are required on HW to perform lighting computations.

Enumerator
Config0 

Reflect Red, Distribution 0, Spotlight.

Config1 

Reflect Red, Fresnel, Spotlight.

Config2 

Reflect Red, Distribution 0/1.

Config3

Config4 

Reflect Red/Green/Blue, Distribution 0/1, Spotlight.

Config5 

Reflect Red/Green/Blue, Distribution 0, Fresnel, Spotlight.

Config6 

Reflect Red, Distribution 0/1, Fresnel, Spotlight.

Config7 

Reflect Red/Green/Blue, Distribution 0/1, Fresnel, Spotlight NOTE: '8' is intentional, '7' does not appear to be a valid configuration.
